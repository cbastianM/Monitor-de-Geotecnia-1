Given the follows values of data from a field pumping drom well of a steady state condition.

To compute for the hydraulic conductivity use the formula from eq. (5.37);

$$
k = \frac{2.303 \, q \, \log_{10} \left( \frac{r_1}{r_2} \right)}{\pi (h_1^2 - h_2^2)}
$$

Substitute the values to equation;

$$
k = \frac{2.303 \cdot 0.68 \cdot \log_{10} \left( \frac{60}{30} \right)}{\pi (5.6^2 - 5^2)}
$$

$$
= 0.0236 \, \frac{\text{m}}{\text{min}}
$$

Since the problem requires for the hydraulic conductivity to be in $ \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}} $, convert the obtained value.

$$
k = 0.0236 \, \frac{\text{m}}{\text{min}} \times \frac{100 \, \text{cm}}{1 \, \text{m}} \times \frac{1 \, \text{min}}{60 \, \text{sec}}
$$

$$
= 0.0393 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$
