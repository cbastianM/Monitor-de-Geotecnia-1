**Paso 1**  
1 de 4

Given all the data from the constant head permeability test in the sand sample.

a.) For the hydraulic conductivity of the sample use the formula from eq. (5.15),

$$
k = \frac{QL}{Aht}
$$

$$
= \frac{580 \cdot 0.35}{125 \cdot 0.420 \cdot 180}
$$

$$
= 0.0215 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

before substituting the given values to the formula, make sure that all the units are in uniform.

**Paso 2**  
2 de 4

b.) Solving the seepage velocity of the sand using the formula from eq. (5.10)

$$
V_s = \frac{V}{n}
$$

Since the value of the velocity is unknown, solve using the formula from eq. (5.6)

$$
V = ki
$$

solve for the value of the hydraulic gradient use formula from eq(5.13)

$$
i = \frac{h}{L}
$$

$$
= \frac{420}{350}
$$

$$
= 5.13
$$

So,

$$
V = ki
$$

$$
= 0.215 \cdot 5.13
$$

$$
= 0.0258 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

**Paso 3**  
3 de 4

The value of porosity is not present in the given data, therefore compute for the value of porosity using the given void ratio $ e = 0.61 $,

$$
n = \frac{e}{1 + e}
$$

$$
= \frac{0.61}{1 + 0.61}
$$

$$
= 0.38
$$

Compute for the value of seepage velocity,

$$
V_s = \frac{V}{n}
$$

$$
= \frac{0.0258}{0.38}
$$

$$
= 0.068 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$
**Resultado**  
4 de 4

$$
\text{a.) } k = 0.0215 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

$$
\text{b.) } V_s = 0.068 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$
