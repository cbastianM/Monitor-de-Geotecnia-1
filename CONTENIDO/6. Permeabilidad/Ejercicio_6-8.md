$$
k \propto \frac{e^3}{1 + e} \tag{1}
$$

**Paso 2**  
2 de 3

Compute for the hydraulic conductivity at a void ratio of $ e = 0.48 $ using the formula eq. (1);

$$
k \propto \frac{e^3}{1 + e}
$$

$$
\frac{k_{0.62}}{k_{0.48}} = \frac{\left( \frac{0.62^3}{1 + 0.62} \right)}{\left( \frac{0.48^3}{1 + 0.48} \right)}
$$

$$
\frac{0.03}{k_{0.48}} = \frac{0.147}{0.075}
$$

$$
k_{0.48} = 0.0153 \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$
