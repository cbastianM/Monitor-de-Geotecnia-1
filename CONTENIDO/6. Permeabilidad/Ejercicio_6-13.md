**SOLUTION:**

Based on the given *figure (5.24)*, and the given *height* of each soil, and the *coefficient of permeability* of each layered soil.

Solve for the Ratio of the Vertical Equivalent hydraulic conductivity to the Horizontal Equivalent hydraulic conductivity.

$$
\frac{K_{V(eq)}}{K_{H(eq)}} \tag{1}
$$

For the Horizontal equivalent hydraulic conductivity, using the formula from Eq.(5.30).

$$
K_{H(eq)} = \frac{1}{H} (K_{H_1} H_1 + K_{H_2} H_2 + K_{H_3} H_3 + \dots + K_{H_n} H_n) \tag{2}
$$

For the Vertical flow using the formula from Eq.(5.35):

$$
K_{V(eq)} = \frac{H}{\left( \frac{H_1}{K_{V1}} \right) + \left( \frac{H_2}{K_{V2}} \right) + \left( \frac{H_3}{K_{V3}} \right) + \dots + \left( \frac{H_n}{K_{Vn}} \right)} \tag{3}
$$

Solving for the total height $ H $ of the layered soil

$$
\sum H = H_1 + H_2 + H_3 + H_4
$$

$$
= 1 + 1 + 1 + 1
$$

$$
= 4 \text{ m}
$$

**Paso 2**  
2 de 3

Substitute the obtained values of $ H $, $ H_n $, and $ K_n $

For Horizontal Flow from eq. (2),

$$
K_{H(eq)} = \frac{1}{H} (K_{H_1} H_1 + K_{H_2} H_2 + K_{H_3} H_3 + \dots + K_{H_n} H_n)
$$

$$
= \frac{1}{4} \cdot (10^{-3} \cdot 1 + 2.0 \times 10^{-4} \cdot 1 + 10^{-5} \cdot 1 + 2.0 \times 10^{-3} \cdot 1)
$$

$$
= 8.025 \times 10^{-4} \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

and, For Vertical Flow from eq. (3),

$$
K_{V(eq)} = \frac{H}{\left( \frac{H_1}{K_{V1}} \right) + \left( \frac{H_2}{K_{V2}} \right) + \left( \frac{H_3}{K_{V3}} \right) + \dots + \left( \frac{H_n}{K_{Vn}} \right)}
$$

$$
= \frac{4}{\left( \frac{1}{10^{-3}} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{2.0 \times 10^{-4}} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{10^{-5}} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{2 \times 10^{-3}} \right)}
$$

$$
= 3.76 \times 10^{-5} \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

Solving for the Ratio of the horizontal equivalent hydraulic conductivity ($ K_{H(eq)} $) to the value of the vertical equivalent hydraulic conductivity ($ K_{V(eq)} $) from eq.(1)

$$
\frac{K_{V(eq)}}{K_{H(eq)}} = \frac{3.76 \times 10^{-5}}{8.025 \times 10^{-4}}
$$

$$
= 0.0469
$$

---

**Resultado**
