Given the table in problem the values of the void ratio and the hydraulic conductivity of the normally consolidated clay.

To compute for the hydraulic conductivity of the clay with a void ratio of $e = 0.62$. Use the formula from eq. (5.28).

$$
k = C \left( \frac{e^n}{1 + e} \right) \tag{1}
$$

To solve for the value of the porosity to be use in the eq. (1), use the formula;

$$
\frac{k_1}{k_2} = \frac{\left[ \frac{e_1}{1 + e_1} \right]}{\left[ \frac{e_2}{1 + e_2} \right]} \tag{2}
$$

**Paso 2**

Substitute the values from the table in the problem to compute for the porosity;

$$
\frac{k_1}{k_2} = \frac{\left[ \frac{e_1}{1 + e_1} \right]}{\left[ \frac{e_2}{1 + e_2} \right]}
$$

$$
\frac{1.2 \times 10^{-6}}{3.6 \times 10^{-6}} = \frac{\left[ \frac{0.8^n}{1 + 0.8} \right]}{\left[ \frac{1.4^n}{1 + 1.4} \right]}
$$

$$
\frac{1.2 \times 10^{-6}}{3.6 \times 10^{-6}} = \left( \frac{0.8}{1.4} \right)^n \left( \frac{2.4}{1.8} \right)
$$

$$
n = 2.48
$$

use the formula from eq. (1)
