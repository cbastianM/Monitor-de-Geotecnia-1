**Paso 1**  
1 de 4

From the obtained values of the variable permeability test, the formula from eq. (5.18);

$$
k = 2.303 \frac{aL}{At} \log_{10} \frac{h_1}{h_2} \tag{1}
$$

Before substituting the known values to the equation, units of the values should be in uniform.

---

**Paso 2**  
2 de 4

a.) Compute the hydraulic conductivity using eq.(1)

$$
k = 2.303 \left( \frac{aL}{At} \right) \log_{10} \left( \frac{h_1}{h_2} \right)
$$

$$
= 2.303 \left( \frac{0.04 \cdot 0.2}{0.1 \cdot 180} \right) \log_{10} \left( \frac{500}{300} \right)
$$

$$
= 2.27 \times 10^{-4} \, \frac{\text{cm}}{\text{sec}}
$$

---

**Paso 3**  
3 de 4

b.) Compute the head difference when the obtain time is $ t = 100 $ seconds, using the value of the hydraulic conductivity from previous solution (a.). Using the formula eq.(1)

$$
k = 2.303 \left( \frac{aL}{At} \right) \log_{10} \left( \frac{h_1}{h_2} \right)
$$

$$
2.27 \times 10^{-4} = 2.303 \left( \frac{0.04 \cdot 0.2}{0.1 \cdot 100} \right) \log_{10} \left( \frac{500}{h_2} \right)
$$

$$
= 376.4 \text{ mm}
$$
