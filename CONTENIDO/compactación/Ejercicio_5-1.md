### **Paso 1** 1 de 5

We were given with a soil sample with a specific gravity of $G_s = 2.75$. To calculate each zero-air-void unit weight denoted as $\gamma_{zav}$ of the soil in $\text{kN/m}^3$ for the moisture content at $w = 5\%, 8\%, 10\%, 12\%$, and $15\%$, use the following expression:

$$\gamma_{zav} = \frac{\gamma_w}{w + \frac{1}{G_s}} \qquad \because (1)$$

### **Paso 2** 2 de 5

For $w = 5\%$, using the unit weight of water in $\text{kN/m}^3$ which is $\gamma_w = 9.81 \text{ kN/m}^3$, Calculate the zero-air void unit weight, we have:

$$\begin{aligned}

\gamma_{zav} &= \frac{\gamma_w}{w + \frac{1}{G_s}} \qquad \because (1) \

&= \frac{9.81}{0.05 + \frac{1}{2.75}} \

&= \boxed{23.716 \text{ kN/m}^3}

\end{aligned}$$
