### Paso 1
1 de 5

The *in situ* specification of the soil:

$$\gamma_{moist} = 16.5\text{ kN/m}^3$$
$$w = 18\%$$
$$G_s = 2.75$$

This soil is to be excavated and transported to use as a compacted fill with specifications:

$$\gamma_{d(min)} = 16.27\text{ kN/m}^3$$
$$w = 18\%$$
$$V_{compacted} = 7651\text{ m}^3$$

Determine how many cubic meters of soil are needed for the $7651\text{ m}^3$ compacted fill.


### Paso 2
2 de 5

Calculate the dry unit weight of the *in situ* soil $\gamma_{d(in\ situ)}$ using the following relation:

$$\gamma_{in\ situ} = \gamma_{d(in\ situ)} (1 + w)$$

$$\gamma_{d(in\ situ)} = \frac{\gamma_{in\ situ}}{1 + w}$$

$$= \frac{16.5}{1 + 0.18}$$

$$= 13.983\text{ kN/m}^3$$

### Paso 3
3 de 5

Calculate the volume of soil to be excavated from the *in situ* soil to produce a $7651\text{ m}^3$ compacted fill using the following equation:

$$V = \frac{\gamma_{d(compacted)}}{\gamma_{d(in\ situ)}} \times \text{Volume of Compacted fill}$$

$$= \frac{16.27}{13.983} \times 7651$$

$$= 8902.333\text{ m}^3$$

### Paso 4
4 de 5

Provided that truckloads are $178\text{ kN}$ each, determine the number of truckloads needed to transport. Take note that excavating the soil is still in its moist state. Use the following expression:

$$n = \frac{\gamma_{in\ situ} \times V}{178}$$

$$= \frac{(16.5) (8902.333)}{178}$$

$$= 825.216\text{ trucks}$$

$$\approx 826\text{ trucks}$$

### Resultado
5 de 5

$$V = 8902.333\text{ m}^3$$

$$n = 826\text{ trucks}$$
